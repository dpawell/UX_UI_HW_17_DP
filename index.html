My UX Skills

User Research

I value in-depth competitor analysis to make sure our design offers the best option. I also perform extensive surveys and will always double back to get more data if design decisions or insights start to feel like they need more substantiation.

User Empathy

As a neurodivergent person, I’ve always been extremely empathetic by nature. Professionally, I harness my extreme empathy to create designs that centralize the user’s feelings and experience

Qualitative Interviews

I typically conduct seven to ten conversational interviews via Zoom with users, for twenty minutes or more. For my GrandTotal app research, I conducted interviews for almost fifty minutes per-person in order to get the clearest picture possible of their travel pain points.
